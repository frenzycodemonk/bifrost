Welcome to SJBHS

Founded by fathers of the Society of Jesus, a Roman Catholic order of priests, our school carries almost 500 years of pedagogic history. Jesuit educational institutions around the world groom pupils to become good citizens and valued members of society who will make a positive difference.

The motto of the school - Fide et Labore - urges every Josephite to scale new heights in learning, sports, service and give generously of himself in the service of others. Our inspiration is that simple man, St. Joseph of Nazareth, the foster father of Jesus, whom the Lord God led in faith utilizing his toil to bring to fulfillment the divine plan.
